A short biography of Galen Weston

Willard Gordon Galen Weston was born in Marlow, Buckinghamshire, near London, england. He was born the youngest of nine children. In 1961, Weston moved to Dublin, Ireland to start up a grocery store. By 1965 he had six stores and four years later, he bought a a bankrupt store and renamed it Penney’s. A year later four more of his stores were open and two years after that, eleven more stores opened, with a few outside of Dublin. In 1966 he married Hilary Frayne. In 1972 he moved to Canada to help his dad, Garfield Weston, with his business, Lawblaws. He was named the CEO of Loblaws companies. By 2002 he was the second richest man in Canada and by 2004 he was the 46th richest man in the world.

Innovation

Galen Weston started up a chain of grocery stores in Dublin, Ireland. His business kept growing and expanding, later taking over a department store, naming it Penney’s. The expanse never really stopped. It kept growing and growing. Later he took over his fathers company and became even more successful. Years later, he opened up the first NoFrills, wich feature loblaws new brand NoName.

Galen Westons entrepreneurial skills

Galen Weston has many entrepreneurial skills and here are a few

  • He is not afraid to take risks.
  • He has very good money management skills.
  • He is a hard worker.
  • He is motivated.
  • He has an ambition.

These are only a few of the many, important entrepreneurial skill that successful entrepreneurs need and Galen Weston has.

Contributions

His company, Loblaws Companies Limited, has worked with many charitable organizations to give back to the communities where they operate. They partner with Food Banks Canada to help the 800 000 people who go rely food banks for food, all across Canada. They have also worked with environmental organizations such as the World Wildlife Foundation (WWF) to inspire Canadians to take action for the environment. They’ve also work with many other organizations to raise awareness for childhood obesity. They have impacted the future by trying to sell healthy food and helping people in need.
